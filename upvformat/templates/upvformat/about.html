The Project

This project, titled "UPV Markup Language to Moodle Converter," is the result of months of dedication and hard work. It addresses a practical need in the field of e-learning by simplifying the process of creating and converting question banks from the UPV Markup Language to a Moodle-compatible format. The tool I developed aims to save educators time and effort, allowing them to focus more on teaching and less on the technical challenges of exam preparation.

The project is designed to be user-friendly, efficient, and highly functional, offering features such as multiple-choice and numeric question conversion, user account management, and easy-to-navigate interfaces. The application leverages the Django framework for back-end development and integrates various third-party tools to enhance its capabilities.
